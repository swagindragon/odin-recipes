Beef and Broccoli stir fry

Goes well with that Rice recipe.

A great fusion of protein and vegtable with a great flavor profile. Generally used with Beef Carne Asada.

Ingredients

  • Beef Carne Asada
  • Broccoli
  • Oyster sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Mince garlic
  2. Cut Broccoli into eating portions
  3. Cut the beef into eating portions
  4. Toss the garlic with the oil into a wok or pan and set it to medium heat
  5. Once garlic is golden brown throw in broccoli
  6. Once broccoli is soft to liking throw in the Beef
  7. Apply oyster sauce when meat is cooked to liking
  8. Profit?
