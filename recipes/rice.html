Rice

Rice is commonly eaten throughout the world as a sidedish or can be used in a main dish. This type of rice shown above is called Jasmine rice.

Ingredients:

Only ingredient needed is rice and a rice cooker
  • Rice

    • Steps:

    1. Grab as much rice as needed
    2. Wash rice in a clean bowl with cold water and then drain 2-3 times
    3. put washed rice into rice cooker and fill with 2 parts water to the amount of rice put in
    4. start rice cooker
    Once rice is finished cooking, stir it up a bit with a spoon or laddle.

    Serve accordingly
