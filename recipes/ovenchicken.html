Ovened Chicken Breast

Chickens are just turkeys that can't fly.

Also a great way eat healthy and lean.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Virgin Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps:

  1. Wash and dry chicken breast.
  2. Sprinkle a bit of oil onto the chicken and then use hands to lather the chicken with the oil.
  3. Add salt and black pepper to taste.
  4. Bake in oven for 380 degrees Farheniet for 40 minutes
  5. $Profit?

Make sure chicken breast is thorughly cooked before eating.
